Build custom agents — without writing a single line of code

Gemini Enterprise's no-code Agent Designer lets anyone in your organisation — from marketing to finance — build, test, and deploy AI agents in minutes. Just describe what you want it to do in plain English.

1Describe your agent's goal in plain language
2Connect it to your data sources — Drive, SharePoint, CRM, ERP
3Test, refine, and publish to your team's Agent Gallery
Example agent prompt
"Every Monday morning, pull last week's sales data from Salesforce, compare it to our Q2 targets in Sheets, write a 3-paragraph summary for the sales team, and send it to the #sales-updates Workspace channel."
